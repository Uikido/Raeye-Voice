Welcome to Raeye Voice Support

Raeye Voice is a bilingual educational app created to help children learn through interactive activities, audio lessons, and visual learning experiences. This support page brings together help information, app details, and quick ways to contact support in one clean place.

1. About the app

Raeye Voice is designed especially for young learners and children with special needs, including autism, speech delay, and language learning difficulties. The app focuses on simple, engaging learning experiences that are easy to explore with visual and touch-based interaction.

The app is built for parents, teachers, therapists, and caregivers who want a child-friendly learning tool with bilingual and faith-based educational support.

4. Account Deletion Request

Users can request deletion of their Raeye Voice account by contacting us at: tessemat@ttechnos.com.

Please include the email address associated with your Raeye Voice account.

When your account deletion request is processed, we will delete your account email and password/authentication data from our Firebase database.

If you delete the app from your device, locally stored app data will be removed from your device. However, deleting the app alone does not automatically delete your account from our Firebase database. To permanently delete your account data, you must submit an account deletion request.

We do not keep additional account data after deletion unless retention is required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes.
