Privacy Policy for Raeye Voice

Effective Date: May 2026

Raeye Voice is designed to support children’s communication and learning experiences under the supervision of a parent or guardian. We are committed to protecting user privacy and minimizing data collection.

Information We Collect

Raeye Voice collects only limited information necessary for authentication, parental controls, and app functionality.

Information provided by parents or guardians may include:

  • Email address used for authentication
  • Parent authentication credentials through Firebase Authentication
  • Parent PIN or protected settings access
  • Language preferences
  • Optional custom voice recordings or uploaded learning content

The app may also locally store:

  • Learning preferences
  • Selected language
  • Saved categories and icons
  • Parent settings
  • Audio recordings created by the parent

Most learning-related information is stored locally on the device.

We do not store child learning data in Firebase except for authentication-related parent account information required to sign in and manage the app.

Children’s Privacy

Raeye Voice is intended for use by children only under the supervision of a parent, guardian, educator, or therapist.

Children are not permitted to independently create accounts within the app. Parent or guardian accounts are used to manage settings, permissions, and learning functionality.

We do not use children’s information for advertising, behavioral tracking, or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Raeye Voice uses Firebase Authentication provided by Google solely for secure parent account authentication.

The app does not use:

  • Third-party advertising
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Behavioral tracking
  • Data brokers

No personal information is sold to third parties.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Authenticate parent accounts
  • Maintain app functionality
  • Save learning preferences
  • Protect parent-controlled settings
  • Improve app stability and security

Account Deletion

Parents or guardians may delete their account directly from the Parent View section of the app.

For child safety and parental control purposes, account deletion is protected behind parent authentication and cannot be performed by a child user.

When an account is deleted, associated authentication data is removed.

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect stored information, including secure authentication systems, protected parent settings, and standard mobile platform security protections.
