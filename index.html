Privacy Policy for Raeye Voice

Effective Date: May 2026. Raeye Voice is designed to support children’s communication and learning experiences under the supervision of a parent or guardian, with a privacy-first approach, parental controls, Firebase Authentication for parent accounts, and minimal data collection.

At a glance
  • Minimal data collection Only limited information is collected for authentication, parent controls, and app functionality.
  • Parent-supervised use Children cannot independently create accounts or delete parent-controlled accounts.
  • Local learning data Most learning preferences, categories, icons, and recordings stay on the device.
  • No ads or tracking Children’s information is not used for advertising, behavioral tracking, or marketing.

Raeye Voice is designed to support children’s communication and learning experiences under the supervision of a parent, guardian, educator, or therapist.

We are committed to protecting user privacy, minimizing data collection, and creating a safe experience for children and families.

1. Information We Collect

Raeye Voice collects only limited information necessary for authentication, parental controls, app functionality, and learning features.

Information provided by parents or guardians may include:

  • Email address used for authentication.
  • Parent authentication credentials through Firebase Authentication.
  • Parent PIN or protected settings access.
  • Language preferences.
  • Optional custom voice recordings.
  • Optional uploaded learning content.
  • Optional YouTube learning video access initiated by the parent or guardian.

The app may also locally store:

  • Learning preferences.
  • Selected language.
  • Saved categories and icons.
  • Parent settings.
  • Audio recordings created by the parent.
  • Offline communication content.
Most learning-related information is stored locally on the device.

2. Children’s Privacy

Raeye Voice is intended for use by children only under the supervision of a parent, guardian, educator, or therapist.

Children are not permitted to independently create accounts within the app. Parent or guardian accounts are used to manage settings, permissions, learning content, and communication features.

We do not use children’s information for advertising, behavioral tracking, marketing purposes, or the sale of personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

Raeye Voice uses trusted third-party services only for core app functionality.

These services may include:

  • Firebase Authentication provided by Google for secure parent account authentication.
  • Firebase services for app stability and security.
  • YouTube links or embedded educational content selected by parents or guardians.

Raeye Voice does not use:

  • Third-party advertising SDKs.
  • Personalized advertising.
  • Data brokers.
  • Behavioral advertising systems.
No personal information is sold to third parties.

4. How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Authenticate parent accounts.
  • Maintain app functionality.
  • Save learning preferences.
  • Protect parent-controlled settings.
  • Support communication and learning features.
  • Improve app stability and security.

5. YouTube and External Learning Content

Some learning experiences within Raeye Voice may open educational YouTube videos or external content selected by parents, guardians, educators, or therapists.

When accessing third-party platforms such as YouTube, users may also be subject to the privacy policies and terms of those services.

Raeye Voice does not use YouTube viewing activity for advertising or behavioral profiling.

6. Account Deletion

Parents or guardians may delete their account directly from the Parent View section of the app.

For child safety and parental control purposes, account deletion is protected behind parent authentication and cannot be performed by a child user.

When an account is deleted, associated authentication data is removed.

Locally stored data may remain on the device until the application is removed or app data is cleared by the user.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect stored information, including secure authentication systems, protected parent settings, Parent PIN protection, standard mobile platform security protections, and minimized online data collection.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Data Retention

Most user-generated content and learning information remain stored locally on the device unless cloud functionality is introduced in future versions and explicitly enabled by the parent or guardian.

Authentication-related information may be retained as required for account management and security purposes.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new features, security improvements, legal requirements, or app functionality updates.

Updated versions will be posted within the app or associated app store listings.
